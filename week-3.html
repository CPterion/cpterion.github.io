This week started with a meeting discussing all the tools we’ve researched and settled on PyAutoGUI. Since it wasn’t the tool I’ve researched the past week, I had to take time reading the documentation on how to use it. Not only that, but the tasks assigned to me were fairly new. I had little idea on how it works, so I had to look it up before implementing them. I continued the New Employee Onboarding Training as well, learning more about the company.
