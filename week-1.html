Week #1: First Week of Foundations

Steven Jethro Vitangcul

Sat 14 June 2025 • 1 min read

We started the first week with onboarding meetings led by the HR, IT, and Security Team. They introduced us to the Mission, Vision, and Core Values of Open iT which gave us an idea on principles and goals they’re trying to achieve. They also outlined the rules and policies of the company in those meetings. Since we were provided work laptops, they also gave guidelines on the dos and don’ts - like installing programs without permission from the Security Team or changing the security settings. They gave short hands-on tutorials on how to use Microsoft Outlook and Teams. It included training on sending and managing emails, and scheduling meetings - thus making our collaboration with the team more effective and improving our ICT literacy. We also got introduced to the software we’ll be working on and got assignments to test and familiarize ourselves with the topics that were covered.
